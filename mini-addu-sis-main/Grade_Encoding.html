Grade Encoding Guidelines

  1. Grades in the NEW Grading System must be encoded as A,B+,B,C,D,F,FD,WP
  2. Non FYPD Grades in the OLD Grading System must be from 65 to 00
  3. FYPD Grades in the NEW Grading System must be encoded as A,B+,C+,C,D,F,FD,WP
  4. FYPD Grades in the OLD Grading System must be encoded as O - Outstanding, HS - Highly Satisfactory, S - Satisfactory, MS - Moderately Satisfactory, F - Fair, P - Poor
  5. NE Grades will not be accepted. Students who have not taken exams must be provided the minimum grade. After the exam has been taken, the teacher should file a grade correction form at the Registrar's Office.
  6. Select AUD for Audit students.
